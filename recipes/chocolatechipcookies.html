Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is a recipe on how to the best chocolate chip cookies ever!

Everyone should try out this recipe. There are only a couple of ingredients and when they come out the oven, they smell just as good as they taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vailla extra
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Disslive baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocliate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes
  9. Coli on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
